All alumni and former students of the Virginia Union University of Richmond, Va., met at the residence of Mr. and Mrs. Robert F. Tinsley, $310 Prairie avenue, Monday evening at 8:30. The purpose of evening was to form an Alumni Club of all students and other students of Virginia Union. The Rev. J. C.
