## NEXT WEEK AT THE B. W.

Another good vaudeville program has been booked for the Basker Washington Theatre next week. On the bill will be Esther Bigean the talented singing soprano; Ollie Young with an entertaining novelty; Bell and Bell; and Rhodes and Jenkins two crack-a-jack doubles. Monday will be "Pay Day" for the patrons, when every one entering the theatre will receive an envelope containing some sum of money from 1c to $5. Trading stamps to $40 in value or passes. Tuesday nights first show will start just after the Velled Propet Parade passes the doors.
