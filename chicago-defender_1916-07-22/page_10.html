MAKES APPEAL FOR

CASEMENTS RELEASE

Miami, Fla., July 21—Dr. A. P. Holly, formerly of Hayt, is the father of a movement to save the life of Sir Roger Casement (white), who is sentenced to death for high treason in England for bringing in exposes of the Belgian atrocities in the 'Congo rubber country, where some of the most awful crimes of the past years have been committed. While in the consular service he felt as if he had been a victim of the United Kingdom, England or Scotland and Ireland, and had prosecuted the stand he thought was right. For this action he has been sentenced to life imprisonment. The fact that Sir Roger Casement fought so bravely against atrocities in the Congo, Dr. Holly is urging members of the Race to take the initiative in requesting the British ambassador to forward an urgent appeal to the authorities in favor of the benefactor of the Race. Dr. Holly now devote his attention to the Race and appeal to the better class of whites to stop the lynching and " Jim树" raws in the south.
