The Pullman Porter's Bonnet Association services mission Sunday, May 9, at St. Mark church at 4 p.m. to a large and appreciative audience. The meeting last year. Rev. W. S. Braddan was the speaker, and, as usual, his oration was characterized with food for the audience, and largely judged by the department of the Pullman porter. The musical program was rendered by St. Mark on the South; John Smith, who sponsored "SE" and Jacob Lowe, who rendered "Forador's Love Song," accompanied by Mr. Jobson.
