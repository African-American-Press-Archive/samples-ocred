## Fillmore's Resignation

Whether the resignation of Lieut. Col. Charles W. Fillmore will act to better the regiment is not known just now. Fillmore has always been identified with the 15th. Since his enlistment in the national guard in the mid-1870s he has always been connected with the military in some way. His first commission was with the Ninth battalion of Ohio and his last with the new 15th. ## Success Irritates Some People

For some time New York has been flooded with stock-selling schemes of all nature. Most of the promotors of these schemes try to sell their stock by appealing to Race loyalty: pride, joy. The meeting was held civic society. The event was for the enforcement of the two amendments. Handolph and Hinton, were appointed after a motion had been passed to hear a resolution to be sent to every congressional member of the United States and to both the Republican and Democrat national conventions at Chicago and Sun Francisco.
