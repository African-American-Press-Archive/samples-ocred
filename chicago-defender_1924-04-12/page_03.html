The Race population of approximately 500 began dispersing last Tuesday, when it received a report that all the settlement would be fired.

All day and night families were seen going to railroad stations and boarding trains for New Orleans and Shreveport. Some who had the fare gathered their few belongings and left for the North. Others became to mask the homes of Race people as soon as they left, taking everything of any value that was left behind.

White business men of this city tried to help the people from leaving, and are now facing a labor shortage.
