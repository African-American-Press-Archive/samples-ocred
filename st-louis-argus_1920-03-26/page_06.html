Omega Psi Phi Fraternity In Atlanta

(Associated Negro Press)
ATLANTA, Ga., March 24—The Eta Chapter of the Omega Phi Psi fraternity has been organized among the Negro college men of this city. Their has arrived from headquarters in Hoover plans are on foot for securing a fraternity house for the local chapter.

The Omega Pal Phi fraternity was chartered in 1912 and members among its members some of the most distinguished men of the race. Among this number may mentioned Colonel Charles Young, ranking colored officer in the United States army and more recently appointed United States military attaché to the American legalian at Liberia. M. H. Thoreau has been elected as bailiffs of the Eta Chapter.

(Associated Negro Press) ATLANTA, Ga., March 24—The Eta Chapter of the Omega Phi Psi fraternity has been organized among the Negro college men of this city. Their has arrived from headquarters in Hoover plans are on foot for securing a fraternity house for the local chapter.
